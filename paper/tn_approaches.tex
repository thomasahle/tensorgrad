  \fi}

\textbf{B3: Khatri--Rao fan --- does not work.} Two attempts (\texttt{branch
down sep}, explicit \texttt{yshift}s) could not make the \texttt{graphs}
placement engine center a fan-out/fan-in between two triangles; the branch
nodes land on or below the chain axis and downstream nodes misalign. Verdict:
approach B is pleasant for \emph{chains} (B1 composes fine with \texttt{\char`\\dloop}),
but branching shapes should use approach A or D.
